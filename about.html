Company Overview

Earning the right

FOODGUARD, which went live in 2020, was set up by a bunch of young, engineering enthusiastic graduates as an online retailing venture with the objective of making essential needs accessible to individuals living at home that too from their trusted local shops.

FOODGUARD is the India's first open e-commerce marketplace connecting merchants and customers online. It offers over 10 million products cross 20+ categories including vegetables, fruits, medicines, books, grocery, beauty products etc. It also offers 20+ services including plumber, carpenter, painter, mechanic, driver, real state srvices and many more.
