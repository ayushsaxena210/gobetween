Frequently Asked Questions

Q1.  Why should I sell on FOODGUARD?
A1.  One should sell on FOODGUARD because we connect you to sell your goods directly to the customer.

Q2.  How do I sell on FOODGUARD?
A2.  To sell on FOODGUARD:

  • Register yourself at FOODGUARD app.
  • Once an order is received on your timeline ,just check the availability at your store and send Accept request to the customer.
  • Once customer verified that he will buy from you then pack the product and deliver to the customer.

Q3.  Who can sell on FOODGUARD?
A3.  The merchant who sells the product lying under the category of Vegetables,Medicine, Fruits, Groceries, Household Items.

Q4.  Can I offer both products and services on FOODGUARD?
A4.  Absolutely yes, the person who lie under the category such as Electrician, Plumber, Mechanic, Driver, Tutor can give the service to the customer.

Q5.  Can we provide rent facility of car and house on FOODGUARD?
A5.  Yes ,one who has car or house can give it to the rent to the person in need.

Q6.  Will I get charged for listing products on FOODGUARD?
A6.  No. Listing of products on FOODGUARD is absolutely free. FOODGUARD does not charge anything for listing your catalogue online.

Q7.  Who decides the price of the products?
A7.  As a seller, you will set the price of your products.

Q8.  Who takes care of the delivery of my products?
A8.  You will only take care of the delivery of your product to the customer.

Q9.  How will I get paid
A9  You will directly get paid by the customer after the delivery of the product

Q 10.  Do I need any documents to register on FOODGUARD
A 10.  No you don't need any any documents to register on FOODGUARD. You just need to mention your Shop name, GST Number, Product Category.

Q 11.  When can I start selling?
A 11.  After registering as a merchant you can start listing your products and start selling.

Q 12  How many listings are required to start selling?
A 12.  You are required to have a minimum of 1 listings(unique products) to start selling on Flipkart.
